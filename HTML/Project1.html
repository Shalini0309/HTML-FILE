Essential Travel Tips

Before you Go

  1. Passport & Visas

    Research visa requirement for your destination

  2. Health Precautions

    Visits a travel clinic 4-8 weeks before departure.

    Check vaction requirement and health advisories.

  3. Travel Insurance

    Purshase comprehension travel insurance that covers :

    • Medical emergencies
    • Trip cancellation
    • Lost luggages
    • Emergency evacuation

Packing tips

Packing Cubes
Organize your suitcase and save space by using packing cubes to separate different types of clothing.
Universal Adapter
Bring a universal power adapter if traveling to multiple countries with different outlet types.
Offline Maps
Download offline maps of your destination before departure to navigate without internet access.
Documents
Keep digital and physical copies of important documents like your passport, insurance policy, and itinerary.

Currency Exchange Tips

Avoid exchanging currency at airports and hotels, as they typically offer the worst rates.

Use local ATMs to withdraw cash in the local currency for better exchange rates.
